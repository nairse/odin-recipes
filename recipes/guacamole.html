Jackie's Guacamole Recipe

Description

Guacamole has always been one of my favorite dishes to prepare. It's quick, it's easy and only requires a handful of ingredients. I loved my recipe but it was not until my SO created her own version of it that I realised mine wasn't so good afterall. Her recipe was way simpler and required even lesser ingredients. Follow along if you want to prepare the best homemade guac that you ever tasted!

Ingredients

  • Avocado 1 large
  • Squeezed lime 1 tbsp
  • Red onion chopped 1/4 cup
  • Garlic powder 1 tsp
  • Chopped Cilantro 1 cup
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Cut the Avocado into a bowl and mash them to chunks or puree if that's how you like
  2. Add the chopped red onion into the bowl and give it a good mix
  3. Add the lime juice, garlic powder, salt and pepper, mix well
  4. Finally add the chopped cilantro and give it another mix
  5. Enjoy your easy and simple guacamole with tortilla chips
